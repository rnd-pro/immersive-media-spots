Interactive Media Spots

A modular Web Components library for immersive media experiences — 360° views, comparisons, panoramas, video, 3D models, and more.

v1.0.0
Spinner Diff Gallery Panorama Video 3D Model Audio

ims-spinner 360°

360° product spinner with drag-to-rotate interaction. Supports adaptive resolution, autoplay, motion blur, and cover image.

ims-diff Compare

Image comparison slider — drag divide line left/right to compare two images. Ideal for before/after, A/B testing, or visual QA.

ims-gallery Gallery

Multi-image gallery with adaptive loading. Navigate through high-resolution images with toolbar controls.

ims-pano 360° Pano

360° panorama viewer powered by Three.js. Drag to look around, with adjustable field of view.

ims-video Video

Video player with multiple quality sources, HLS support, subtitle tracks, and progress bar.

ims-model New

3D GLTF/GLB model viewer with orbit controls, auto-rotation, and zoom. Powered by Three.js.

ims-audio New

Audio waveform player with real-time Web Audio API visualization, seek, and playback controls.
